ABOUT

Bella Pao is an energetic student who loves design. She looks at the world through a curious lens and upside down perspective. She specializes in branding, visual design, motion graphics, and user experience.
Outside of design projects, Bella Pao enjoys skiing the mountains of Colorado, skateboarding with friends, and covering the local music scene through the lens of her camera.
Constantly inspired by her surroundings, Bella Pao keeps an open mind and curious thought which drives her to design a better world for others.
Bella Pao is a student at the University of Colorado Boulder studying Strategic Communications with an emphasis in Media Design and a minor in Creative Technology and Design. Passionate about photography, music, and design this website is an excercise in HTML and CSS built with Github.
