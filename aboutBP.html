Introducing Bella Pao!

A Dreamer and A Doer!
In constant wonder of life, she utilizes her curiosity and creativty to design for others. With a background in visual communication, her big picture thinking allows her to maintain detail oriented work for creative solutions in design and stategic communication. Growing up by the Florida seashore and taking risks in the Colorado mountains, the love of live adventures keep her adaptive in every situation and welcoming to new experiences.
Currently working public relations for the outdoor industry.
Free time is devoted to concert photography, skiing the winter POW, biking for joy, and trying the local pumptrack on her carver. She also has a strong passion for biodesign and circular materials in textiles.
